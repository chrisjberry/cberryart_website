Welcome to my art website. Every piece shown here is hand-painted by me. I’m drawn to the Futurist idea of simultaneity — the compression of memory, present impression, and anticipation into a single image. My Lost In The Forest series comprises 24 paintings in which I invite you to step into a surreal world where nature intersects with memory and perception.

About Chris Berry

By day I work in academia as a cognitive psychologist. Painting is how I spend the quiet hours, building up each piece half an hour at a time, night after night.
